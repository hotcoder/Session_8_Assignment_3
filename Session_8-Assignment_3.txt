Problem:

You take the SAT and score 1100. The mean score for the SAT is 1026 and the standard
deviation is 209. How well did you score on the test compared to the average test taker?


Ans)


Calculate Z value = (your score - avg score)/std = 0.354

by using z-table we will get 0.1368 for this we need to add 0.5 , so we will get 63.68% (when converted to percentage)

So  63.68 percentage of test-takers scored below you
